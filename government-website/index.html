Welcome

You have been summoned for jury duty!


It may seem like an inconvenience. It may conflict with your vacation plans. You may worry about its impact on your job. But keep in mind that jury service is a rare opportunity to make a difference, to serve your community and to play a crucial role in America's system of justice.

Today, three out of four Americans believe the jury system is the most fair method of determining guilt or innocence in criminal cases, according to an American Bar Association survey.

Another survey found that a large majority of federal judges say they would prefer to have a jury rather than a judge decide their case if they were ever on trial.
